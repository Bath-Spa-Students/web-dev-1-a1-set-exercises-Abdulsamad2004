Overview

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series created by the Duffer Brothers. Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, the plot follows a group of kids who encounter supernatural forces and government conspiracies when a young boy disappears under mysterious circumstances.

The series is known for its nostalgic references to 1980s pop culture, compelling storyline, and talented cast.

Visit Official Website

Plot

In the town of Hawkins, Indiana, a young boy named Will Byers disappears under mysterious circumstances. As his friends and family search for him, they encounter a girl with psychokinetic abilities known as Eleven, who has escaped from a nearby government laboratory. As they uncover the truth behind Will's disappearance, they encounter supernatural creatures from a parallel dimension called the Upside Down.
