Overview

Stranger Things is a science fiction horror series created by the Duffer Brothers. Set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana, the plot follows a group of kids who encounter supernatural forces and government conspiracies when a young boy disappears under mysterious circumstances.

The series is known for its nostalgic references to 1980s pop culture, compelling storyline, and talented cast.
