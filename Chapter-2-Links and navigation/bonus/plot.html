Plot

In the town of Hawkins, Indiana, a young boy named Will Byers disappears under mysterious circumstances. As his friends and family search for him, they encounter a girl with psychokinetic abilities known as Eleven, who has escaped from a nearby government laboratory. As they uncover the truth behind Will's disappearance, they encounter supernatural creatures from a parallel dimension called the Upside Down.
